## Math

The $k^th$ eigenvalue $\lambda_k$ of the compactly resolved quadratic form $q$ is characterized by the min-max formula
$$ \lambd_k = \operatorname{min}_{E^k\subset H} \operatorname{max}_{u\in E^k} \frac{ q(u,u)}{\| u \|^2} $$
